Meet the owner, Sheri Cox

Discover the captivating allure you've been seeking at Frame-Art Gallery, a community-based and independent institution committed to delivering unparalleled Art and Frame solutions.

With a commitment to providing unmatched Art and Frame solutions, we have been serving the Richmond area since 1975. During this time, Frame-Art Gallery has established itself as a trusted source for all your art and framing needs.

We are proud to be a Certified Micro-SWaM vendor in Virginia, demonstrating our dedication to excellence and ensuring that our valued customers receive the highest quality services and products.
