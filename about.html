Meet the owner, Sheri Cox

Happy customers is our mission!

Frame-Art Gallery, a community-based and independent institution, offers an enchanting allure that will captivate your senses. Since its establishment in 1975, this family-owned gallery has been devoted to delivering unparalleled Art and Frame solutions. With a steadfast commitment to excellence, we strive to provide exceptional services and top-notch products, ensuring that our esteemed customers always receive the utmost quality experience.

We offer a diverse range of authentic artwork and prints that are conveniently accessible at our physical location. Numerous pieces are not displayed on our website due to regular updates in our inventory. We cordially invite you to visit our store and explore our extensive assortment of artwork.

We are proud to be a Certified SWaM vendor in the state of Virginia.
