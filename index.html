Meet the owner, Sheri Cox

Frame-Art Gallery, a community-based and independent institution, offers an enchanting allure that will captivate your senses. Since its establishment in 1975, this family-owned gallery has been devoted to delivering unparalleled Art. With a steadfast commitment to excellence, we strive to provide exceptional services and top-notch products, ensuring that our esteemed customers always receive the utmost quality experience. We offer a diverse range of authentic artwork and prints.
