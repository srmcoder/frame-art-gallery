Introduction

It's not just the frame, it's the framer.

Discover the WOW factor you've been looking for at Frame-Art Gallery, a nearby, autonomous Micro-SWAN certified Art and Frame boutique.

We offer a wide variety of Original Art, Prints, Posters, Limited Editions, and Fine Art Pieces featuring African-American, Contemporary and Traditional Art.

Our extraordinary designs cater to all types of art, whether they are original masterpieces, shadowboxes or beloved postcards. If you require assistance with the arrangement, we are more than willing to collaborate with you in order to create a design that will be most satisfying to you.

Bring your photographs, prints, or even three-dimensional keepsakes to us, and witness the remarkable transformation of your walls into vivid and significant masterpieces.
